The statement on this page describes how Avian collects and uses the information provided by you. This document will also describe the choices given to the user regarding the use of personal information.

Our Ideology

Avian is a company devoted to providing the user the best experience possible. We strive for excellence, but we also understand that the best user experience is different for everyone. To achieve our goals, we will give the user a choice about where their information is held.

Information Policy

Avian Apps will always prompt the user for information, however, the user always has a choice of where their information is stored. As such, the user will have the choice to save their data locally on the device, on a 3rd party cloud server, or even our very own servers

Sharing your Information

Locally

Avian does not have access to this information, as it is secure on your device.

3rd Party Server

Avian does not accept responsibility for any information stored on a server that is not ours.

Avian holds the right to share information stored on our servers

Third Party Services

Our applications reserve the right to include third party services that will allow us to track application usage and stability. We accept no responsibility for how these third parties use this data.

Tracking Policy

We or third party services used by us may track usage statistics from application use. the usage statistics will be used to improve the application in use. we aggregate all user information to gain a better understanding of how our applications are used, to improve our marketing efforts, and provide content that we believe the user wants.

Ads

Ads may appear in various Avian applications. The companies used may send the end user cookies, these cookies allow the advertisement server to give you targeted adds. Avian holds no responsibility on the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Security

Avian strives to keep all data secure on our servers and especially during communication. However, no security system is truly secure. Due to this, Avian accepts no responsibility for any security breaches.
